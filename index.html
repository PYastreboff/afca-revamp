Australian Financial Complaints Authority

Fair, free and independent resolution for financial complaints

We help consumers and small businesses resolve disputes with financial firms — at no cost, with no bias, and with outcomes you can trust.

Independent & impartial

We operate independently of government, regulators and the financial industry.

Free for consumers

There is no charge to lodge or pursue a complaint with us.

Timely resolution

We work with both parties to resolve complaints fairly and efficiently.
