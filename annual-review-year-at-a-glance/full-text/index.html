Complaints

  • 100,745 complaints received. Down 4% on 2023-24
  • 101,302 complaints closed. Down 3% on 2023-24
  • The average time to close a complaint was 79 days ¹
  • 29,117 Open cases. ²
  • $390,862,905 in compensation was awarded to consumers through AFCA’s dispute resolution processes
  • 4,764 complaints about financial difficulty received
  • 4,648 small business complaints received
  • 11,091 complaints excluded due to being outside AFCA’s jurisdiction ³
  • 4,817 small business complaints closed
  • 5,035 financial difficulty complaints closed
